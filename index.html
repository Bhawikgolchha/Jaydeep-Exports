02 — The Story

Six decades of clay, fire and craft.

Founded in 1967, National Potteries began as a small family kiln in Morbi — Gujarat's ceramic heartland. Today, under the Jaydeep Exports banner, we ship roofing tiles, terracotta jali blocks, cladding bricks and our flagship Biletral decorative collection to architects, builders and homeowners across India and overseas.

Every block leaves the kiln after a 1,200°C burn — naturally fungus-free, asbestos-free, paintable, and tested to last twenty years through Indian summers and monsoons alike.
